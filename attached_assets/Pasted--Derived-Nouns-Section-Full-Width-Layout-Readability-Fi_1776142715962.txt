**Derived Nouns Section — Full-Width Layout & Readability Fix**

## Scope

UI-only improvement.
Do NOT change data, logic, or generation.

---

## Problem

The **Derived Nouns (الأسماء المشتقة)** section:

* is too narrow (right-aligned cluster)
* leaves large empty space on the left
* cards are small and hard to read
* does not match importance of the content

This makes it feel like a secondary widget instead of a **core learning block**.

---

## Required Fix — Full-Width Expansion

### 1. Section must span FULL container width

* Remove right-side clustering
* Expand section to use full horizontal space
* Align with other main sections (like conjugation table)

---

### 2. Convert layout to responsive grid

Instead of small stacked cards on the right:

Use:

* **Desktop:** 3–4 columns across full width
* **Tablet:** 2 columns
* **Mobile:** 1 column

Cards should **fill the row evenly**

---

### 3. Increase card size and readability

Each derived noun card must be:

* wider
* taller
* with more internal padding

### Improve hierarchy inside each card:

#### Arabic word

* large
* bold
* primary focus

#### Transliteration

* medium size
* clearly readable (NOT faint)

#### English meaning

* readable secondary text
* not tiny or washed out

#### Type label (Exaggeration / Place / Instrument)

* small but clear
* consistent styling

---

### 4. Section structure (clear grouping)

Group visually like this:

#### Exaggeration Nouns (صيغ المبالغة)

→ row/grid

#### Place & Time Nouns (اسم المكان والزمان)

→ row/grid

#### Instrument Nouns (اسم الآلة)

→ row/grid

Each group:

* full width
* clear spacing between groups
* visible section label

---

### 5. Remove “floating card” feeling

Fix by:

* equal spacing between cards
* consistent alignment
* no large empty margins on left
* no compressed right column

---

### 6. Fix gray text inside cards

Apply same contrast system:

* Arabic = strong dark
* transliteration = medium-dark gray
* English = readable gray (not faint)
* metadata = lighter but visible

---

### 7. Visual importance rule

Derived Nouns section should feel:

* equal importance to Verb Forms
* not a side panel
* not compressed
* not decorative

---

## Result Expectation

After fix:

* section fills the page width
* cards are large and readable
* no empty space imbalance
* Arabic is dominant
* English supports clearly
* layout feels intentional and structured

---

Provide:

1. updated full-width grid layout

* reduce number of cards
